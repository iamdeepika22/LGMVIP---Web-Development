About Us

At Coding Bees, our mission is to continuously innovate the best ways to train the next generation of developers and to transform the the way tech education is delivered. Coding Bees was founded in 2021 to bridge the knowledge gap between colleges and industry. Coding Bees boasts of world-class teaching faculty and a state-of-art learning platform for Coding education with faculty alumni of IIT, Stanford, IIIT and Facebook.
